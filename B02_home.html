How to Reserve a Seat

1

Click "Reserve A Seat"

Start your seat reservation process by clicking the button on the left.

2

Confirm Your Location

Select the general area where you want to find a seat.

3

Select Block, Floor and Room

Narrow down your selection by choosing the specific block, floor, and room.

4

Select Your Seat

Choose an available seat from the interactive seat map.

5

Manage Your Reservations

View, modify, or cancel your appointments in "Check My Appointment".

Scan the QR code at the classroom entrance to reserve a seat instantly
